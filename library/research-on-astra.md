# Astra Europa — Research Notes

## 1. Identity & Mission

Astra Europa is a pan-European political movement, founded on **9 May 2026** — Europe Day, the 76th anniversary of the Schuman Declaration. They self-describe as **"liberal, federalist, and forwardist"** — a deliberate three-word branding that positions them to the left of Ave Europa ("federal-right") while remaining clearly pro-European and sovereignty-focused.

- **Mission statement:** *"Europe has the talent, the wealth, the science, and the imagination to flourish. Astra Europa has been founded to harness those strengths and set Europe on a path to the stars."*
- **Positioning:** They explicitly state they "move beyond the tribalism of both left and right." Unlike Ave, they do not claim a place on the classical left-right spectrum — they frame it as a post-ideological, liberal-democratic project.
- **Blueprint:** Their founding materials acknowledge building on the models of **Volt** and **Ave Europa**, but with a "clearly liberal, democratic, and forward-looking platform."
- **Website:** astraeuropa.eu

---

## 2. The Founding Story — and the Ave Connection

This is the most important contextual fact: **Astra Europa was founded by people who left Ave Europa over the far-right infiltration incident.**

- Co-founder **Nikodem Skrobisz** resigned from Ave on **15 March 2026**, publicly alleging that from January 2026, Ave's central organs had been "infiltrated and/or radicalised by far-right forces" — systematically expelling moderate/liberal members and inviting AfD and FPÖ politicians to a General Assembly at Castle Ortenberg.
- Several **Dutch and Polish Ave chapter leaders** departed at roughly the same time.
- Astra Europa launched approximately **8 weeks later**, on Europe Day.

The founding story is therefore the inverse of Ave's: Astra was built explicitly as the alternative for people who believe in the federalist-liberal project but will not tolerate far-right drift. That is their origin myth — and their strongest differentiator.

## 4. Manifesto — 12 Guiding Stars

Released on Europe Day 2026. All 12 now confirmed from full manifesto text (astraeuropa.eu/manifesto).

| # | Guiding Star | Core Position |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **A European Federation** | Sovereign democratic federation with elected head, bicameral parliament, independent judiciary. Subsidiarity everywhere it belongs; national constitutions and monarchies preserved within the federal structure. Immediate push for a constitutional convention — willing to start with willing member states. |
| 2 | **United Defence & Foreign Policy** | Single foreign policy, genuine European defence capability under democratic federal control. Integrated command, procurement, intelligence, industrial base. **European nuclear deterrent** under federal democratic control — explicitly rejects outsourcing the ultimate security guarantee. AI in defence with democratic oversight. |
| 3 | **Europe in the World** | Engage from strength. Support Ukraine "as long as it takes." Reform global multilateral institutions (replace UNSC veto with regional representation). Partner with democracies; invest in developing world as equals, not patrons. |
| 4 | **Borders, Migration, and Justice** | Federal border and migration system. Secure external borders, firm returns. Legal migration calibrated to integration capacity. Asylum via in-region principle (host regions bear primary responsibility). Engineered migration as hybrid warfare — explicitly named (Belarus/Poland example). Federal justice against cross-border crime, terrorism, trafficking. |
| 5 | **Energy Sovereignty** | Rapid transition to clean domestic energy (renewables, nuclear, geothermal). Honest about fossil fuels in the transition — prefers domestic production over imports from hostile regimes. Single European energy market: harmonised permitting, connected grids, joint strategic procurement. |
| 6 | **The Digital Age** | European frontier AI models — no dependency on US or Chinese systems for critical functions. AI will replace "enormous categories" of work; commit to managing this carefully and distributing benefits equitably. Information integrity as democratic precondition. Privacy as a right; **explicitly rejects mass surveillance and government backdoors**. |
| 7 | **Fiscal Sovereignty** | EU budget (~1% GDP) too small to govern. Federal fiscal capacity to match responsibilities; direct tax relationship between federation and citizens, replacing national contributions. Federal corporate tax base to end jurisdiction-shopping. Simpler, more transparent tax systems — rebalance from labour to wealth/ownership. |
| 8 | **Prosperity Through Scale** | Complete the single market in earnest: uniform company law, capital markets union, remove bureaucratic barriers. Single federal rulebook, one-stop compliance, sunset clauses on regulation. European high-speed rail, connected infrastructure. Buy European in strategic sectors (defence, critical infrastructure, semiconductors). Support EU champions to scale without relocating. |
| 9 | **A Human-Centered Economy** | Adopt OECD Better Life Index alongside GDP as official measure. Housing as social priority, not speculative asset. Welfare reform: replace fragmented bureaucracy with simple system that rewards work + guarantees minimum living standard. Strong parental leave, childcare. Honest engagement with demographic transition and pension reform. |
| 10 | **Climate & Sustainability** | Permanent comprehensive carbon border mechanism. Cut emissions without offshoring them. Restore ecosystems, sustainable agriculture, strong animal welfare standards. Europe as world leader in low-carbon economy — treat the transition as industrial opportunity. |
| 11 | **Ambition & Discovery** | Serious investment in fundamental research and the path from discovery to European companies. Public architecture with aesthetic ambition. **"Per Europam ad astra" as literal aspiration** — spacefaring Europe, orbital infrastructure, asteroid mining as strategic frontier. |
| 12 | **European Identity** | Layered identity (local → regional → national → European); outer layers protect inner ones. Cultivate shared European civic belonging: pan-European media, Erasmus expansion, education with European history and languages. Goal: being European feels as natural as being Swedish, Lithuanian, or Greek. |

**Voice of the manifesto:** Substantially more detailed and policy-dense than Ave's 12 priorities. Reads as written by people with serious economic, legal, and technical expertise (which the team profile confirms). The register is technocratic-liberal: "calibrated," "empirical fact," "OECD Better Life Index," "carbon border mechanism." Far from civilisationist.

**Two positions worth flagging as bold/potentially controversial:**
- The **European nuclear deterrent** (Star 2) — explicit, not hedged. Most European parties avoid this.
- **AI displacing "enormous categories" of work** (Star 6) — unusually honest about the scale of transformation. Politically risky; also differentiating.

**What they explicitly reject:** "green degrowth, failed socialist experiments, and tribal identity politics fetishizing ancient myths." A deliberate triple rejection — against the green-left, the old socialist left, and the identitarian right simultaneously. Clean triangulation that leaves room to the right of Volt but firmly blocks the far right.

## 6. Partnerships & Network

Astra explicitly names **Volt** and **Ave Europa** as the blueprints it builds on — which implies potential for coordination or a future three-way scene (Volt, Ave, Astra) as the liberal-federalist space.

**NXT4EU** — the clearest confirmed partner and Astra's most significant immediate asset. Founder Odin is on the Interim Steering Committee. NXT4EU is a pan-European social media advocacy platform active on X (@NXT4EU, **26,461 followers**, 7,600+ posts since September 2024) and Mastodon, promoting federalist positions on defence, digital sovereignty and AI. Before the March 2026 split it was integrated into Ave Europa's communication channels; it moved to Astra at launch. NXT4EU gives Astra an existing, engaged audience from day one — without which Astra's own social presence would be nearly invisible.

Their Dutch founding density also connects them naturally to the broader Netherlands-based European movement ecosystem. No other external partnerships publicly confirmed at time of writing.

## 8. Risks & Unknowns

- **Very early stage.** Launched 7 weeks ago. No track record, no governance tested under pressure, no established funding base. The interim committee has a 6-month mandate — structural decisions ahead.
- **Funding unknown.** No public information on financing. Likely personal capital + early donations, same as Ave's initial model. May also be thinner than Ave (no founders who "quit their jobs and invested savings" cited yet — team seems employed).
- **Membership figures unknown.** 1,000+ social followers ≠ 1,000 members. No membership numbers disclosed.
- **The four missing guiding stars.** Could reveal unexpected positions on cultural identity, family policy, or other sensitive areas.
- **Founder bandwidth.** Most founding members appear to hold full-time jobs. Capacity for rapid scaling is unclear.
- **Relationship with Ave.** No public statement of rivalry or collaboration. Given the founding story, some tension is latent; overt antagonism would damage both.
