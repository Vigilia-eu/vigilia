What drives us

Not idealism. Urgency.

We are not building this because Europe is romantic. We are building this because democracy is in genuine danger, and the tools to protect it at scale do not yet exist in the right hands.

Authenticity

Russia scales with bots. We scale with real people, real editorial voice, real outlets. That asymmetry is our moat. The moment we compromise it, we become what we counter.

Persistence

Russia has been at this for fifteen years. We are building for the same horizon — decentralised, sovereign, self-improving. Not a campaign. A permanent watch.

The red line

Any partner or node whose editorial voice drifts toward identitarian or far-right framing is disqualified. We amplify what we believe in. That constraint is load-bearing.
