The system

How it's wired

Content doesn't move one way. It travels in three directions.

↓Distribute. The core researches a story once; every hub re-voices it. ↑Contribute. A hub catches a story of its own and sends it up, where the core coordinates it. ↔Syndicate. That story then travels sideways, hub to hub, each re-voicing it for their people.

Down, up and sideways: the story circulates. That is what makes it a federation, not a broadcast tower.
